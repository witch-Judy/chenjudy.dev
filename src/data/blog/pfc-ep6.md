Episode 6 of the interactive Prefrontal Cortex (PFC) series.

You worked all day, collapsed on the couch to scroll short videos, and felt even more exhausted afterward. The next day you went for a walk in the park instead — and your mind felt clear. That's not a fluke. Real rest isn't shutting your brain off — it's rotating the shift crews. This episode explores the alternation between the TPN (Task-Positive Network) and DMN (Default Mode Network).

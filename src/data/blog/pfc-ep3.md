Episode 3 of the interactive Prefrontal Cortex (PFC) series.

A four-year-old's war with a marshmallow left traces in brain scans forty years later. This episode explores the neuroeconomics of self-control — the signal competition between hot and cold systems, how temporal discounting turns your future self into a stranger, and self-control strategies that don't rely on willpower.
